Create token locker contract (BEP-20)

Build a token locker smart contract for (BEP-20) tokens lock, team tokens vesting.

Your tokens will be locked in the contract for required time, after that they can be withdrawn back to your wallet.

Once your token locking contract is created, you will be able to download your own UI (HTML file) or use our UI as a showcase of your token lock.

(Mainnet and Testnet available)
